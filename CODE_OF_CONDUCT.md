# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation 
in this project and our community a harassment?free experience for 
everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, 
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level 
of experience, education, socio?economic status, nationality, personal 
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment for 
our community include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.
- Showing empathy toward other community members.

## Unacceptable Behavior
Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

- Harassing or discriminatory comments, slurs, or insults.
- Threats of violence or encouragement of self-harm.
- Public or private harassment, including unwelcome sexual attention.
- Publishing others’ private information without explicit permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussion or other deliberate interference 
with normal discussion.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior 
may be reported by contacting the project team at horstmann.marc@trendverlag.de. 
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a 
response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. 
The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard 
to the reporter of an incident.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies both within project spaces and in public 
spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community 
(e.g., on social media, in online chat channels, or at conferences).

## Appeals
If you feel you were wrongly accused of violating the Code of Conduct, 
you can contact the project team at horstmann.marc@trendverlag.de to 
appeal the decision.
